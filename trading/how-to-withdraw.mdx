title: "How to withdraw"
icon: "inbox-out"
---

You can withdraw USDC and native tokens from 1st at any time.

- USDC is always withdrawn on Base
- Vested tokens are withdrawn on their native chain

**Withdrawing funds**

1. Navigate to your Assets page and click Send
2. Paste the external wallet address you want to receive funds on
3. Select the asset you want to withdraw
4. Confirm the withdrawal

Your funds will appear in your external wallet within seconds.
